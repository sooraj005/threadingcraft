Threading

The art of threading involves the gentle and precise twisting of a length of cotton thread along a row of hair, which is then removed. The twisting thread traps the hair, lifting it out of the follicle and it is then removed. Threading allows for greater precision overall compared with tweezing and waxing for the achieving exactly the desired effect.
